# MP Forest Cover Analysis

I built this project while learning Pandas and NumPy. Since I'm also preparing 
for the MP Forest Officer exam, I thought — why not analyze something relevant?

## What I did

Looked at forest cover data for 25 districts in Madhya Pradesh and tried to 
find which districts are doing well and which ones are far behind the 33% 
national target set by the Forest Policy of India.

## What I found

- Only 10 out of 25 districts actually meet the 33% target
- Dindori and Balaghat are the most forested (52% and 48%)
- Bhind and Morena are at the bottom — just 2% and 6%
- Central and Eastern MP have much better forest cover than the North and West

## Tools used

- Python, Pandas, NumPy
- Jupyter Notebook

## What I practiced

groupby, sort_values, np.where, value_counts, describe — basically all the 
core pandas and numpy operations I had just learned.

## Data
Based on Forest Survey of India (FSI) published data.
